Who We Are

About Objective: Cancer

A student-led movement making cancer education accessible to everyone.

🎓 Registered 501(c)3 Nonprofit Organization

Objective: Cancer is a student-led nonprofit focused on raising awareness for cancer in all its forms and promoting proactiveness in early detection and prevention.

We believe that knowledge saves lives. Through our Outreach Initiative, social media campaigns, and community events, we make sure life-saving cancer information reaches people where they are.

Learn More About Us

Our Mission
To educate communities about cancer — its forms, risk factors, early detection, and proactive health decisions.

Cancer By The Numbers

Facts that drive our mission — backed by research.

1 in 2
Men in the US will develop cancer at some point in their lifetime.
Source: American Cancer Society
1 in 3
Women in the US will develop cancer at some point in their lifetime.
Source: American Cancer Society
20M+
New cancer cases are diagnosed globally each year, a number that continues to rise.
Source: World Health Organization
30–50%
Of all cancer cases are preventable through lifestyle changes and early action.
Source: World Health Organization
Up to 90%
Survival rate improvement is possible with early detection for many cancer types.
Source: American Cancer Society
#1
Tobacco use is the single largest preventable cause of cancer death worldwide.
Source: CDC
40%
Of cancer risk can be reduced by maintaining a healthy diet, exercise routine, and avoiding known carcinogens.
Source: World Cancer Research Fund
600K+
Americans die from cancer each year, making it the second leading cause of death in the US.
Source: American Cancer Society
