Who We Are

About Objective: Cancer

A student-led movement making cancer education accessible to everyone.

🎓 Registered 501(c)3 Nonprofit Organization

Objective: Cancer is a student-led nonprofit focused on raising awareness for cancer in all its forms and promoting proactiveness in early detection and prevention.

We believe that knowledge saves lives. Through our Outreach Initiative, social media campaigns, and community events, we make sure life-saving cancer information reaches people where they are.
