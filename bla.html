Donation Drive Highlight

Our most recent project — assembling care packages with handwritten cards for cancer patients.

Care Packages for Cancer Patients & Families

Care packages are a huge part of what we do at BLA — because beyond raising awareness, we want to directly support cancer patients and their families in any way we can.

We've already completed a donation drive for the Dana-Farber/Brigham Cancer Center at South Shore Health, delivering care packages filled with personal items and handwritten gift cards from our members.

And we're not stopping there — we're currently working on our next donation drives for Hope Lodge and Beth Israel. Stay tuned! 🎗️
